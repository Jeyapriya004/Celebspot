About the Project

Welcome to our hackathon project, where we explore the fascinating world of computer vision and facial recognition. Our goal is to develop a tool that can accurately identify famous celebrities in photos.

Facial recognition technology has gained significant popularity with the rise of Artificial Intelligence. We are harnessing this technology to create a device that characterizes and identifies well-known individuals in images.

How It Works

Our project involves building a facial recognition model capable of detecting and distinguishing faces in photographs. This model is trained using a dataset of images featuring specific celebrities.

The steps include:

  1. Collecting a comprehensive dataset of images for each celebrity.
  2. Training the facial recognition model using machine learning techniques.
  3. Testing the model's performance by providing images for identification.

Our Mission

Our mission is to showcase the capabilities of facial recognition technology and provide a practical application that demonstrates its potential.

We aim to offer an educational experience for beginners in the field, helping them understand the fundamentals of computer vision and machine learning.
